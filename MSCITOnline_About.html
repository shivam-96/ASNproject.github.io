Frequently Asked Questions

What is MS-CIT Online Mode?
"MS-CIT Online" is special edition of MS-CIT course where learner can do the learning at his/her home computer in truly online mode. Learner will access eLearning content of MS-CIT course on his/her home computer with internet connection. Learner will have to install MKCL’s World Class Learning Management System (LMS) – ERA on his home computer.
How can I take admission for MS-CIT Online Mode?
There are two options to take admission for “MS-CIT Online” mode. Learner can visit www.mkcl.org/join and take online admission by payment of course fee online. Learner will have to select nearest MS-CIT Authorized Learning Center (ALC).
Learner can also visit/contact nearest MS-CIT Authorized Learning Center (ALC) and take admission through the center.
What is minimum configuration of home computer required?
For taking admission for “MS-CIT Online” mode, learner needs to have a home computer with internet connection. Minimum hardware configuration of learner’s home computer is as follows:
  • RAM: Minimum 4 GB
  • Operating System : Windows 7 or above
  • Hard Disc: Minimum 20 GB free space on one of the available drives
  • Internet: Active internet connection is required.
  • Minimum free RAM: 2 GB (before launch of ERA)

Learner should confirm availability of minimum hardware on home computer before confirming admission for MS-CIT Online.

Is internet connection required for learning through MS-CIT Online?
Yes. Active and continuous internet connection is required for MS-CIT Online course. 4G or Broadband connection is preferred for best user experience.
What is the fee I need to pay for taking admission for MS-CIT Online?
link
If I am not able to make online fee payment, how can I take admission for MS-CIT Online?
You can take admission through one of the nearest MS-CIT Authorized Learning Center (ALC) for taking offline admission.
How do I get started with my MS-CIT Online Learning?
You need to install MKCL’s Learning Management System i.e. ERA on your home computer after taking admission for MS-CIT course. Contact your selected MS-CIT Center for further guidance in this regard.
Do I get MS-CIT Book if I take admission for MS-CIT Online?
Yes, every confirmed learner of MS-CIT Online course will get physical copy of MS-CIT book from his/her selected MS-CIT Center.
Can I change my learning mode from MS-CIT Online to MS-CIT at Center later?
No. Admission once confirmed for MS-CIT Online mode cannot be changed to MS-CIT at Center mode later.
What are minimum marks that I need to score to be eligible for final examination?
Learner needs to score minimum 20 marks out for 50 internal marks AND learner needs to complete minimum 40 sessions out of 50 learning sessions in MS-CIT course.
Learner needs to score minimum 20 marks out for 50 internal marks AND learner needs to complete minimum 40 sessions out of 50 learning sessions in MS-CIT course.
MS-CIT course has two varieties. MS-CIT Job Readiness and MS-CIT IT Awareness. Learner has to choose one of these varieties at the time of admission. Contact your selected MS-CIT Center for detailed counselling session on this.
What if I am stuck at any concept in my course?
For any support during the period of your MS-CIT Online course, contact your selected MS-CIT Center.
Can I access MS-CIT Online course on my mobile?
No, MS-CIT Online course cannot be accessed through mobile.
Can I access MS-CIT Online Course from any device?
MS-CIT Online course can be accessed only through the home computer registered at the time of admission.
What if I face any technical issue in my login?
For any support during the period of your MS-CIT Online course, contact your selected MS-CIT Center.
What is MSBTE?
MS-CIT Final Examination is conducted by Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education (MSBTE).
How will be my final examination of MS-CIT Online Course is conducted?
MS-CIT Final examination will be preferably conducted at your selected MS-CIT center. Final decision allocation of exam center is taken by MSBTE.
How much minimum marks do I need to score to clear MS-CIT Final Examination?
Learner has to score minimum 20 marks out of 50 marks to clear MS-CIT Final examination.
Who will issue my MS-CIT Certificate?
MS-CIT Final Certificate is issued jointly by MKCL and MSBTE after successfully clearing internal assessment as well as final examination.
How I will get my MS-CIT Final Certificate after final examination?
If learner has scored minimum marks in both internal marks and final examination, Final MS-CIT Certificate will be issued by MSBTE. Final certificate will be handed over to learner by selected MS-CIT center at the time of admission.
